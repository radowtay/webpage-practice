While a child Rachel loved science and math. Her mother quickly taught her to love reading, and her father taught her to love music. Most of her work experience has been in the field of music education, starting to teach private lessons only 2 years into college. This gives her music career 12 years, with over 6 years teaching public school orchestra and 25 years of playing cello. A push came to change careers, and is now headed into data analytics and web design. The most exciting prospect of this career change is that work now feels like continuously solving jigsaw puzzles, while still getting to be creative and use mathematics.
