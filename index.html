Newly trained data analyst and web builder! Since December, I have been enrolled in a Data Analytics Bootcamp offered through Rice University. We change units every week, with major projects and data assignments due on a different topic/language/environment each week. Much of this has depended on the ability for the student to be self-sufficient in learning and practicing new programs and languages using their own devices. Please view my Projects or Gallery pages, or go straight to my GitHub.

While a child Rachel loved science and math. Her mother quickly taught her to love reading, and her father taught her to love music. Most of her work experience has been in the field of music education, starting to teach private lessons only 2 years into college. This gives her music career 12 years, with over 6 years teaching public school orchestra and 25 years of playing cello. A push came to change careers, and is now headed into data analytics and web design. The most exciting prospect of this career change is that work now feels like continuously solving jigsaw puzzles, while still getting to be creative and use mathematics.

Whether I am working directly with your data, or building the front-end, I will take to it quickly and efficiently. I am a fast learner and work well in groups or alone. Since my education thus far has been on the topics of many things at once, I am capable of being a flexible asset to suit your needs.
